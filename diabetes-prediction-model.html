About Project

The objective of this project is to classify whether someone has diabetes or not. The dataset consists of several Medical Variables(Independent), such as Pregnancy, Glucose, Blood Pressure, Skin Thickness, Insulin, BMI, DiabetesPedigreeFunction, Age, and Outcome Variable(Dependent). The outcome indicates whether a person has diabetes or not. It also shows which algorithm predicts the result with accuracy.
